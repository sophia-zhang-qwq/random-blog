This guide explains how to set up a working VPN for beginners. 
The idea is to use GlaDOS as the client provider(proxy provider/subscription provider), and Clash Verge as proxy client(GUI). Clash Verge imports your subscription URL, manage your proxy confirguration, and routes your network traffic through the selected proxy(You can think of Verge as a intermediate network distributor for your url). For Android/Win you can also substitute to other Clash variants.

---

# Step 1: Pay

- Register a [GlaDOS](https://glados.network/) account. 
- At Subscription&Management, switch the default area and save your personal url.
- Most listed Clash are not working but you can try.

![Step 1](/random-blog/assets/vpn/vpn-1.png)

---

# Step 2: iOS
- For iPhone&iPad, download ShadowClash on app store. 
- Open ShadowClash, Top-right + button to add QR code/file/url in Step 1.
- Connect！


![Step 2](/random-blog/assets/vpn/vpn-2.jpg)

---

# Step 3: MacOS

- For MacOS mac chips, most of the listed Clash are not well maintained. MacOS intel chips maybe work.
For eg, ClashMac involves download mihomo separately(mihoyo start:D),replace url locally and move to core. But ClashMac and mihomo are badly documented, with system compatibility not as described and need to by-pass security issue. FLClash are only for intel and win etc.
Highly recommend [Clash Verge](https://github.com/clash-verge-rev/clash-verge-rev)
- Doanload [Clash Verge](https://github.com/clash-verge-rev/clash-verge-rev/releases) according to your operating system at github.
- Open Clash Verge,
    - enter your url on Subscription.
    - open System Proxy.
- Connect!

![Step 3](/random-blog/assets/vpn/vpn-3.png)
